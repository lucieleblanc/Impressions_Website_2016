about

Impressions is an innovative and student-directed photography magazine created to be an outlet for MHS students to share their photography and design with the community. Impressions relies solely on student art submissions to provide the best visual experience. Our magazine not only offers students a creative platform from which to work, but serves as a source of inspiration that pushes individuals to see the world from a more inventive perspective. The magazine has recently received three different awards from the American Scholastic Press Association: First place for Publication Design, Best Design/Photography Magazine, and Outstanding Design Portfolio.
