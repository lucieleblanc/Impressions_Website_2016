UPLOAD


Impressions is created using images submitted by students and alumni of the MHS photography and design department. Submissions are selected by our art editors, a student-run council that works to produce the final copy of each issue.

If you are interested in having your work featured, please submit your images to submissions@mhsimpressions.com, along with your name and phone number. We are constantly looking for innovative work to include in our issues.
